Projects

Hospital Management Website

The "Hospital Management Website" project aims to streamline the administration and patient care processes within a healthcare facility. It includes features such as appointment scheduling, patient record management, billing, and staff rostering. The website ensures efficient communication between staff, patients, and administrators, ultimately enhancing the overall operational efficiency and patient experience in the hospital.

Smart stick for blind people

A "Smart Stick for Blind People" is an assistive device designed to enhance the mobility and independence of visually impaired individuals. It integrates sensors, GPS technology, and haptic feedback mechanisms to detect obstacles, provide real-time navigation assistance, and improve the user's awareness of their surroundings. This innovative project aims to empower the blind community by making navigation safer and more efficient.

Vehicle Classification using ML

"Vehicle Classification using ML" is a machine learning project aimed at automating the categorization of vehicles based on input images or sensor data using dataset "Cifar-10" dataset. It involves training a model to recognize and classify various types of vehicles such as cars, trucks, motorcycles, and bicycles. This technology can be applied to traffic management, surveillance, and smart transportation systems to enhance efficiency and safety.
